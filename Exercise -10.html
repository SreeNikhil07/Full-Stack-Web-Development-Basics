Special Characters Example

Here are some special characters:

  • Less than symbol: <
  • Greater than symbol: >
  • Ampersand: &
  • Copyright symbol: © 2024
  • Registered trademark: ® My Company
  • Trademark: ™ My Product
  • Quatation: "Nikhil"
  • Section Symbol:§Amrutha
  • Euro sign: €Currency
